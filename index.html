About

The Daily Star is once again organising ICT Awards to recognise and celebrate the extraordinary achivements in the field of information and communication technology. The award will be given at a gala event on December 27, 2017.


Objectives:

  • Recognising the excellent works in ICT sector which will bring positive changes to our society.
  • Encouraging the ICT community to strengthen ICT entrepreneurship ecosystem and creating an environment of encouraging and sustaining the entrepreneurial spirit.
  • Highlighting the magnitude of capacity that exists in the country by highlighting the nomineesâ€™ ICT success stories.
  • Supporting the young ICT entrepreneurs to scale up their potentials as a sustainable business model.

Nomination

 

General Criteria:

  • 1. Company has to be registered in Bangladesh
  • 2. In case of JV, minimum 50% Bangladeshi ownership

ICT Solution Provider of the Year (Local Market Focus)

Software and ITES Solution provider (including BPO) that has been providing customized software/ITES solutions to the local market.

Award Criteria: Last 3 years revenue.

ICT Solution Provider of the Year (International Market Focus)

Software and ITES Solution provider (including BPO) that has been providing customized software/ITES solutions to the International market (ICT export).

Award Criteria: Last 3 years revenue

ICT Product of the Year

Software as a product (including mobile apps)

Award Criteria: Number of users; Merit/Value Addition

E-Business of the Year

Any .com initiative, including eCommerce, education portal, online newspapers, job portals, etc.

Award Criteria: Number of users; Value Addition; Last 3 years Revenue

ICT Startup of the Year

Any ICT Startup that is between 1-3 years old and has a fully functional product or service

Award Criteria: Last 3 years revenue; merit/value addition

ICT Business person of the Year

Award Criteria: 15+ years contribution to the ICT Industry

Selection Process

1. All nomination must be submitted within 15th November, 2017.

2. An independent jury panel will evaluate the nominations and select the winners.

3. Awards will be given at a gala event on 27th December 2017.
